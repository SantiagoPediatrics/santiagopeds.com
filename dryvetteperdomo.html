Yvette Perdomo, M.D.
Board Certified Pediatrician

Dr. Perdomo graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a degree in biochemistry, then graduated from the University of California, Irvine—College of Medicine. She completed her residency training in Pediatrics at the University of Nevada School of Medicine. She is board certified by the American Board of Pediatrics. During her spare time, she enjoys spending time with her family, playing roller hockey, and being a fan of Star Wars.
