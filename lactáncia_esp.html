Lactation (Breastfeeding) Support

Amamantando es la manera natural alimentar a un infante e al niño joven. Los niños que amamentan tienen menos riesgo de varios problemas de la salud, incluyendo asma, alégerias, hospitalización, y menos dificultadades de la digestión. Las madres que amamentan tienien menos riesgo de cancer del pecho, ovário, útero, menos presión de la sangre, menos colesterol, y menos riesgo del desarrollo de la diebetes.

Muchas veces, amamamantando ocurre naturalmente y fácilmente. Pero algunas veces hay dificultades. El bebé puede tener problemas agarrando el pecho para comer. Puede causar a la madre dolor cuando el bebé está amanantando.

Sometimes breastfeeding is easy, but often it is difficult. Signs of trouble include having nipple pain for more than several seconds after your baby latches, nipple bleeding, or your baby not gaining enough weight. If you are having trouble with feeding, or are worried about how your feeding is going, let us help. Our pediatricians can help with basic feeding issues, and Lisa Bretches, our pediatric nurse practitioner, is a experienced lactation consultant. Please call us if you are having trouble with breastfeeding, for example, if you are having pain that last more than a few seconds after your baby latches.

If you are ever worried that your baby is not getting enough to eat, please make an appointment for a weight check so we can make sure.
