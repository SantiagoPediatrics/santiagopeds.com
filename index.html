Welcome to Santiago Pediatrics!

Santiago Pediatrics is a pediatric medical practice committed to providing high-quality pediatric care to children and young people from birth through age 22.

Services

Santiago Pediatrics provides primary medical care by board certified pediatric providers for children and young people ages birth through 22.

Some services we provide:

  • Newborn Checkups
  • Well Child Checkups
  • Vaccinations
  • Breastfeeding help/Lactation
  • Teen Checkups
  • Sports Physicals
  • Sick Visits for Illnesses
  • Developmental Evaluations
  • Standardized Developmental Screenings powered by CHADIS.
